Preparing for Your Visit

Before Your Appointment

  • Complete all required forms
  • Bring your insurance card and photo ID
  • List all current medications and dosages
  • Write down questions for your doctor
  • Arrive 15 minutes early for check-in
  • Bring a family member or friend if needed

During Your Visit

  • Be honest about your symptoms
  • Ask questions if you don't understand
  • Take notes or ask for written instructions
  • Discuss any concerns about medications
  • Request copies of test results
  • Schedule follow-up appointments

After Your Appointment

  • Review your visit summary
  • Fill prescriptions promptly
  • Follow care instructions carefully
  • Keep track of symptoms or side effects
  • Call if you have questions or concerns
  • Mark follow-up appointments on calendar

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring to my first visit?

Bring your insurance card, photo ID, completed new patient forms, medication list, and any relevant medical records.

How do I get my medical records?

Submit a medical records release form, available online or at our facility. Processing typically takes 3-5 business days.

Can I bring someone to my appointment?

Yes, you're welcome to bring a family member or friend for support. Please inform the front desk upon arrival.

What if I need to cancel my appointment?

Please call us at least 24 hours in advance to cancel or reschedule your appointment.
