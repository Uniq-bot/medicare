Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring to my appointment?

Please bring your insurance card, photo ID, a list of current medications, and any relevant medical records. Also bring payment for any applicable copay or deductible.

Do you offer cost estimates before treatment?

Yes! Contact our billing department at least 48 hours before your appointment to request a cost estimate based on your insurance coverage.

What if my insurance denies my claim?

We will work with you to appeal the denial. Our billing specialists can help you understand why the claim was denied and assist with the appeals process.

Can I pay my bill online?

Yes! You can pay online through our secure patient portal 24/7. Simply log in to your account and navigate to the billing section.

Do you accept patients without insurance?

Absolutely. We offer self-pay options and may provide discounts for uninsured patients. Contact our billing department to discuss payment arrangements.
